Work

In my role as an Associate Data Analyst at Tesco, I specialize in modeling, data cleaning, and deriving actionable insights. Notably, I've achieved a 93% accuracy rate in topic modeling on Glassdoor review data, enhancing employee satisfaction and organizational performance. Additionally, I've contributed to the development of machine learning models, leveraging SQL, Python, and Excel for data manipulation and analysis.

Outside of my core responsibilities, I've led projects including chatbot development and face mask detection using pretrained models like VGG16 and ResNet50. I've also created the commodity price detection model across various categories like Laptop,Car and Phone. These experiences, along with certifications in Data Analysis and Machine Learning from Imarticus Institute, highlight my commitment to continuous learning and innovation.

My dedication to excellence extends to achievements like securing a top 10 rank in the JPNG Hackathon event. I'm driven by a passion for leveraging data-driven insights to inform strategic decision-making and drive business growth.

Project Showcase

Face Mask Detection

Developed a face mask detection system using pretrained models VGG16 and ResNet50. Achieved an accuracy of 89% in identifying whether individuals are wearing masks or not. The system helps enforce safety protocols and contributes to public health efforts during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Chatbot

Developed a chatbot using a sequence-to-sequence model, which utilizes natural language processing techniques. The chatbot is capable of engaging in conversations with users and providing responses based on the context of the conversation. Achieved an accuracy of 75% in generating appropriate responses. The chatbot serves as a virtual assistant, offering assistance and information to users in various domains.
